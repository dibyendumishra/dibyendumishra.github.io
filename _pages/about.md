Hi! I am researcher and data science practitioner who is interested in studying power structures in society and using data science methods (both novel and old!) for helping make them more equitable. 

I am currently a Data Scientist with the [Ministry of Rural Development (GOI)](https://rural.nic.in/en) where I work as a member of it's Data and Insights Unit. My work focuses on studying, desgining and building data driven algorithmic solutions (mostly hybrid human-AI systems) using participatory methods towards equitable distribution of resources and services amongst the most underserved populations in India living in rural areas and helping improve the state's capacity through decision aid systems. A large part of my work also involves thinking about the political economy in which such technology and data operate in, which I am always excited to talk about!

In the past, I've worked at [Microsoft Research India](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-india/) under [Prof. Joyojeet Pal](http://joyojeet.people.si.umich.edu/) on projects around political polarisation, crisis informatics and disinformation on social media using computational methods. The focus of these projects revolved mostly around the political self of the understudied non-political class of actors, broadly categorised as influencers, who yield immense influence due to their following and reach on these platforms. The work has been shared through publications (at CSCW, COMPASS, ICWSM, SMSociety, HICCS etc), media articles and blog posts.
